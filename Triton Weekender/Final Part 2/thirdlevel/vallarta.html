Vallarta Express

Vallarta Express is hands down the best place to go to for Mexican food. Just a quick drive down Genesee Ave. and you'll be on your way to enjoy the best burritos at the best price. And if that wasn't enough, this place is open 24 hours!

UCSD students say:

"Vallarta's has the best Carne Asada fries!" - Andy G
"Their California burrito is to die for. What also rocks is that this place is only a 10 minute drive from campus. I love getting a midnight dinner with my roommates!" - Megan S.
"They have the friendliest servers that always make my night!" - Guan Wang

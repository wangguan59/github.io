Point Loma

Point Loma Light House is one of the original eight Cape Cod influenced structures built along the California coast to aid sea navigation.

UCSD students say:

"This is a great little lighthouse to that is fun to walk in and see the history. The funnest part is the beach right off the shore with tide pools that you can search for sea life. " -Connie.C
"Point Loma is one of many great places to watch an amazing sunset. Plus you can see the black cloud over Tijuana from there and if you are anything like me that is reason enough to never go there." -Kelley C.
"Excellent views, peaceful, quiet, contemplative, I could plop myself down anywhere on the grounds and just watch the water exist and the tide's break. " -Brad T.

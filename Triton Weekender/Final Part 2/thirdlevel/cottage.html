The Cottage

The Cottage is an phenomenal location to grab a morning meal or late breakfast. The atmosphere is so bright and warm. It's incredibly easy to feel comfortable at this place. The food also is delicious, fresh, and has that home-made feeling to it. This unique restaurant is a gem you can find in La Jolla.

UCSD students say:

"Perfect place to sit around on a sunday afternoon to enjoy a brunch with loved ones." -Pakie S.
"I LOVE The Cottage. What can I say that hasn't already been said? Basically, classy breakfast for when you want to be classy." -Natalie F.
"Probably one of the best brunch spots I've been to in a long time. Not only is the food good, but the service and the atmosphere are hard to beat. This is a perfect place to impress any out of town guests, me being one of them." -Brenda L.

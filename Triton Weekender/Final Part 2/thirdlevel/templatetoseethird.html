Balboa Park

Balboa Park contains a variety of cultural attractions including museums, theaters, gardens, shops and restaurants as well as the world-renowned San Diego Zoo. They also offer discounted Museum tickets for UCSD student

UCSD students say:

"The air is fresh, the weather is great, plus you have the rose garden, the organ hall, museums, a wonderful sculpture cafe, and the arboretum. " -Stacy D.
"Lots of great museums! Free parking too.It is definitely worth walking around, even if you do not visit one of the museums. The gardens are always changing." -Cel.P

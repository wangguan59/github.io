Inferno

With some of the best talent in San Diego, Inferno is the place to go when you want to dance throughout the evening.

UCSD students say:

"I LOVE this place! The music never disappoints and I always dance from the moment I get there until it closes shop around 1:45 am. The DJs are always on point and the LED wall makes the whole experience really fun." -Vanessa T.
"This place is the house of house music! If I'm in the mood to dance my booty off to some house DJ, this is where I go." -Maria G.
"The DJs were both fantastic, and the whole crowd appeared to be highly energized and having a great time. It was crowded but not insane, and the music was loud enough to feel the bass beats thumping away in your chest, which is exactly what I was looking for on NYE." -Jon B.

Outback Adventures

Through out the year Outback Adventures offers amazing trips that can fit anyone's style. Whether you like surfing, kayaking, rock climbing, hiking, backpacking, horseback riding, snowboarding, or any outdoor activity we have what you are looking for. Even if you have no outdoor experience, Outback Adventures has a trip for you.

UCSD students say:

"I joined this club back when I was at UCSD. I really enjoyed it because they took us on trips, planned everything and were responsible as far as hwo to have a good time and safe time. The prices are good too, geared toward students. "-Peter Y.
"There is a good mix of beginning and advanced routes. It was surprisingly almost empty when I went " Liz M.
"The friendly staff are great in any of their multiple facets. No experience is necessary for the challenge course, climbing gym, or any of their outdoor excursions." -Brian B.

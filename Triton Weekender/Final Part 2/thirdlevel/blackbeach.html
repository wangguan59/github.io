Blacks Beach

Black's Beach is a secluded section of beach beneath the bluffs of Torrey Pines, and it is perhaps the largest nude beach in the United States.

UCSD students say:

"You get the viewing pleasure of watching the paragliders as they draft overhead. So many colors!"-Justin R.
"I never imagined myself going to a nude beach or going nude for that matter, but I have to tell you that it was pretty liberating and I didn't get any tan lines! "- Julia P.
"The scenery is gorgeous. The view from the top before you descend the cliff is wonderful. The hike up and down the cliffs gives you a little bit of a work out. " -Jenn C.

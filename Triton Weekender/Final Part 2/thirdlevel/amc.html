AMC La Jolla

The proximity and venue of AMC La Jolla offers more than just a movie experience, but a whole evening of entertainment.

UCSD students say:

"The theater is a little older but all things considered, still the best deal." Carey W.
"Being at UCSD without a car, I don't have much of a choice where to go, but this theater is so close and the price is pretty good for a broke college student. Perfect for when me and my group of friends want to watch a movie any night of the week." Symphony K.
"The location of this theater is pretty good for every student at UCSD, my friends and I get a quick bite to eat at one of the locations nearby and then head to the theater (good prices) to complete our evening of fun." Bryan C.

Extraordinary Desserts

Extraordinary Desserts has grown to becomes one of the most well known places in San Diego to get your fix of sugary snacks. They're famous for a reason, these desserts truly are remarkable! Of all the choices available, you'd think one wouldn't be all that great- but no, they are all good. They also offer a few brekfast and lunch options to round out the meal. A first choice for anything sugar-related.

UCSD students say:

"I can't believe I allowed myself to live in San Diego this long without giving this place a try! Boy, I sure have been missing out!" - Jamie H
"I'm honestly not a big fan of sweets or desserts, but I highly recommend checking this place out next time you're in San Diego!" -Nick G.
"I love desserts and this place has the best cakes ever, enough said. " -Kat T.

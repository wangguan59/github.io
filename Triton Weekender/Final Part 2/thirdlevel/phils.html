Phil's BBQ

Phil's BBQ is the primier restaurant to visit if you want great-tasting barbecue. Phil's has been featured on food network and is consistenly ranked as one of the top places to get barbecue. Truly a masterpiece. Only a short drive down teh I-5 freeway from campus.

UCSD students say:

"I never get sick of the food here and it always exceeds my expectations! The baby-back ribs & beef ribs are out of this world delicious!" -Mike T.
"This place is always packed during meal times because its that good. But don't get discouraged. The line may be around the corner but the line moves pretty quick and its worth it!" -Arthur C.
"Phil's BBQ is just too good. I would never choose to go out to eat BBQ until I came here for the first time a few years ago. The wait is super long, but it is definitely worth it!" -Gene C.

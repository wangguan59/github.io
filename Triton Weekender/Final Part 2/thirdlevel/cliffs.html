the Cliffs

Scripps Cliffs is a place where the coastline has fallen into the sea, leaving behind a series of gorgeous cliffs abutting a row of beach houses in a crowded residential area near the edge of town.

UCSD students say:

"I've been to a few beaches before, but not one where I can just stand there with the sandy soil and the curious seagulls and forget where I need to be. "-Taric W.
"Watching a sunset at Sunset Cliffs is literally second behind a sunset in Hawaii. Breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean, waves crashing over a canvas of pinks, oranges, and blues..This is one of the most amazing views ever."-Helen L.
"The coastline here is stunning and rugged in the sense of the true sublime. The waves are always crashing against the cliff face, and the sound of the waves slapping the rocks and spraying is quite hypnotic. "-Jen.s

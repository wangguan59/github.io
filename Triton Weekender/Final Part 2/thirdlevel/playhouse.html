Playhouse

La Jolla Playhouse is a Tony Award-winning professional non-profit theatre on campus. UCSD students can often obtain cheaper tickets for many great shows.

UCSD students say:

"I have always had a fantastic time at La Jolla Playhouse. I have been very impressed with the shows that I've been able to see over the course of the past 10yrs." Colleen C.
""It is comparatively cheaper to attend plays here versus the Old Globe, but they both have their charms." Patrick S.
"So whether you are interested in the La Jolla Playhouse in general, or their production of Xanadu specifically, I would give this my highest recommendation. Take a date for a fun romantic night out" -Jon B.

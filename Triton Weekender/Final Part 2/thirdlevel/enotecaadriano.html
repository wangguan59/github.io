Enoteca Adriano

Enoteca Adriano is a marvelous place. It can easily meet your needs. If you want a casual/upscale dining experience, Enoteca Adriano can do it. If you want to come for a formal date, Enoteca Adriano has you covered. This quality alone make it one of the best places to eat. The other reason, is the food. The pasta here is consistently good and the servers really know how to treat their guests, answering questions and meeting any other needs.

UCSD students say:

"The best pasta I've had in my life. Hands down." -Furai X.
"I almost don't want to tell people about this place so I can keep it to myself! Between the Buratta, the Popes Pillow, and the "No Corkage Fee" this place can't be beat!" -Nathan B.
"One of the best restaurants I have eaten at in PB! The atmosphere was great, and the service was exceptional. The owner even came by our table and made sure everything was good. That doesn't happen at most restaurants. On top of that, all the food was delicious." -Michael W.

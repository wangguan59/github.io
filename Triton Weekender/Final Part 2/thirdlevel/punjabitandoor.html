Punjabi Tandoor

Punjabi Tandoor is one of the greatest punjabi styled restaurants we've ever eaten at, if not, the best. This place may not look like much from the outside, but this hole-in-the-wall foodplace is a little piece of heaven. The food is delicious! You will NOT be disappointed.

UCSD students say:

"Oh Punjabi, how you have enlightened me! Your curries fresh, delicious, and not too spicy for me- a perfect match in tastebud heaven." -Mina K
"We have been going here for about 2 years now and have not been dissappointed yet. The food is always fresh and delicious. Great prices, friendly people, great food!!! A MUST GO!!" -Frank M.
"LOVE LOVE LOVE. Probably the best Indian food I've ever had, though I've only gone to a handful of Indian restaurants." -Katie H.

Fashion Valley

Fashion Valley is San Diego's finest collection of stores in a beautiful outdoor shopping center will satisfy your spending craze.

UCSD students say:

"One of my favorite places to shop in SD! They have all the designer stores AND the regular mall stores I tend to check out. They even have dinner at one of my favorite restaurants: Cheesecake Factory! What's not to like?" -Gelene V.
"This mall has all the high end stores you could dream of. All their department stores are fully stocked and their customer service people are great." -Connie M.
"When you move from LA to SD, and don't have a friend in sight... this mall will quickly become your new bff. They have all the stores that your average woman in her 20's and up will love." -Sara K.

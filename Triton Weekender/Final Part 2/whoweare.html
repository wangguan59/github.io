We are the team that is dedicated to providing the UCSD campus with the best there is to do, see, and eat- every quarter. We are a by students, for students group that adventures into the local and greater UCSD areas to find the attractions that will give you the best value for your money We understand what it's like being a student- so we provide you with options that will meet your restrictions; like finding the best things to do that are a short distance away or near an accessible bus stop. Feeling the need to splurge? We cover that area as well- we provide options that are top-notch, whether it be going to a fancy restraunt with a lucky someone, or to a shopping destination to give you retail therapy.
